Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What are the requirements to study in Malaysia?

Most universities require a high school diploma (or equivalent), English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL), and academic transcripts. Specific requirements vary by university and program.

How much does it cost to study in Malaysia?

Tuition fees typically range from $3,000-$15,000 USD per year depending on the university and program. Living expenses are generally affordable at $400-$800 per month.

How long does the visa process take?

Student visa processing typically takes 3-4 weeks after receiving an offer letter. We guide you through every step to ensure timely approval.

Can I work while studying?

Yes, international students in Malaysia can work up to 20 hours per week during the semester and full-time during holidays.

Do you offer accommodation assistance?

Yes, we help arrange accommodation in university hostels or private student housing near your campus.

What is your success rate?

We maintain a 98% success rate in securing admissions for our students to their preferred universities and programs.

Is Malaysia safe for international students?

Yes, Malaysia is one of the safest countries in Asia with low crime rates and excellent safety measures. Universities have modern security systems, and international students are well-protected. Most cities are very student-friendly with good public transportation.

Do I need to renew my student visa every year?

Yes, student visas in Malaysia require annual renewal. The renewal process is handled by your respective university. Your university's international student office will manage all visa renewal documentation and coordinate with immigration authorities to ensure your visa stays valid throughout your studies.

What documents do I need for my application?

You'll need: passport, high school diploma, academic transcripts, English proficiency test scores (IELTS/TOEFL), and passport photo. We provide a complete document checklist and guide you through every step to ensure your application is complete and submitted correctly.
